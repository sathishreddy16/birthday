A Letter for You

My dearest,

If I could wrap up every beautiful thing in the world and hand it to you,

I still wouldn't have given you enough.

There are people who walk through life quietly, leaving no mark —

and then there are people like you,

who rearrange the stars just by being in the room.

I've watched you grow through every season of life,

through storms that would break anyone,

through mornings that seemed too heavy to carry —

and every single time, you rose.

You have a laugh that makes rooms warmer,

a heart that holds more love than it shows,

and a kind of courage so quiet

that most people never even notice it — but I do.

On this day, your birthday, I want you to know:

you are not just loved — you are celebrated.

Not for what you do or what you achieve,

but simply because you exist, and that is enough.

May this year bring you everything you've quietly wished for,

every door you've been afraid to knock on,

every dream you've kept folded inside your heart —

may they all unfold into something magnificent.

You deserve mornings filled with golden light,

evenings that feel like poetry,

and a love so steady and true

that it becomes the air you breathe.

Thank you — for your patience, your warmth,

for being exactly who you are

in a world that constantly asks people to be less.

You are, and have always been, more than enough.

Happy Birthday, beautiful soul.

This world is better because you're in it. ❤️

With all my love, [Your Name]
